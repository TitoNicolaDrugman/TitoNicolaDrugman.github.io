§ 01 — About

Half researcher,
half systems engineer.

It started with a Bachelor's in Artificial Intelligence — inaugural cohort of a brand-new inter-university programme across Pavia, Milano Statale and Bicocca — where the question was simply how a machine could learn at all. The Master's in Computer Science & Engineering at Politecnico di Milano turned that question practical: now I build the infrastructure that lets AI actually run — on-premises, on constrained hardware, without quietly handing your data to someone else's API.

A semester on exchange at CUHK-Shenzhen (香港中文大学（深圳）) reframed how I think about problems and collaboration. In between, I train industrial robots for a living and explain 19th-century engines to school groups at Milan's science museum — different audiences, same job: make something technical make sense to someone who didn't ask for the details.
