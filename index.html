§ 01 — About

Half researcher,
half systems engineer.

I started with a Bachelor's in Artificial Intelligence — the inaugural cohort of an inter-university programme across Pavia, Milano Statale and Bicocca — chasing the question of how a machine could learn at all. That carried me into a Master's in Computer Science & Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, where I now build the infrastructure that lets AI actually run: on-premises, on constrained hardware, without leaking data to someone else's API.

Along the way, a semester on exchange at CUHK-Shenzhen reframed how I think about problems and collaboration. In between, I train industrial robots for a living and explain 19th-century engines to school groups at Milan's science museum — code isn't the only thing I have to make legible to strangers.
